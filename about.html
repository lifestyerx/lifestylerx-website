Our Story

Meet the visionary behind Lifestyle RX and learn how this movement began.

From Pain to Purpose

Our founder – a nursing student, personal trainer and nutrition coach – was given a second chance at life. Through years of chronic pain and emotional turmoil he discovered the healing power of movement, nutrition and mindset. The lessons learned became the foundation for Lifestyle RX: an integrative approach that recognizes the interplay between physical health, emotional wellbeing and spiritual growth.

His journey is a testament to resilience. It underscores our belief that every setback can catalyze transformation when paired with courage and community. Today, he channels his passion for service through his work as a personal care technician and his studies toward becoming a nurse practitioner. His vision extends far beyond personal training – it encompasses a global network of wellness centers devoted to educating and empowering people from all walks of life.

Vision & Values

Guided by compassion, rooted in science and powered by faith, we are committed to transforming lives worldwide.

A Clinic Without Walls

Lifestyle RX is more than a coaching program – it's a philosophy. We envision a global network of clinics and community spaces where individuals receive personalized fitness and nutrition guidance alongside education in mindfulness, emotional intelligence and preventative health. Our first brick‑and‑mortar holistic clinic will open in the near future, serving as a blueprint for centers around the world.

Every interaction is infused with respect, empathy and excellence. We integrate evidence‑based practice with holistic wisdom, ensuring that our recommendations support both physical outcomes and your larger life vision. We believe that each person is inherently worthy and capable of change, and we exist to walk alongside you on that path.

Journey & Timeline

Our milestones – past, present and future – reflect our relentless pursuit of growth.

  • Early Years: Struggled with chronic pain and searched for answers, discovering the healing power of movement and nutrition.
  • 2023–2024: Became certified as a personal trainer and nutrition coach while working as a personal care tech. Began building a community under the Lifestyle RX brand.
  • 2024–2025: Entered nursing school to deepen clinical knowledge and integrate holistic practices with conventional medicine. Expanded coaching programs and began mapping plans for a holistic clinic.
  • 2026 & Beyond: Become a nurse practitioner and launch our flagship wellness center. Franchise the clinic model globally, empowering practitioners to deliver care rooted in love, science and holistic transformation.
