Choose Your Program

Our tiered coaching programs meet you where you are today and grow with you as you transform. Transparent pricing, clear expectations and real results.

Lite Tier – $97/month

A digital‑only program for the self‑starter who wants guidance without one‑on‑one coaching.

  • Custom macro calculator and habit tracker.
  • Access to our nutrition & habit system portal.
  • Monthly email check‑in with progress review.
  • Community forum with other members for motivation.

Ideal for: Men who are comfortable working independently and need a structured plan.

Core Coaching – $399/month

Our most popular option combining training, nutrition and accountability.

  • Personalized training program updated monthly.
  • Custom macros with bi‑weekly adjustments.
  • Weekly progress check‑ins and habit coaching.
  • Private community group and resource library.
  • Bonus: 30‑minute welcome call and goal setting session.

Ideal for: Men who want structured guidance, accountability and flexibility.

VIP Coaching – $599/month

The most personalized experience for men ready to invest fully in their transformation.

  • Customized workouts delivered weekly.
  • Weekly Zoom or phone coaching sessions.
  • Macro tweaks and meal guidance as often as needed.
  • Mindset coaching and stress management strategies.
  • Priority messaging support and accountability.
  • Bonus: Welcome package including Lifestyle RX gear and guided journal.

Ideal for: Men seeking high‑touch coaching, rapid results and deep mindset work.
